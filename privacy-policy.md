
📄 Privacy Policy – NURVA: Kur’an & Namaz

Last Updated: May 2026

NURVA – Kur’an & Namaz (“the App”) respects user privacy and is committed to protecting user information in accordance with applicable laws and Google Play policies.

1. Information Collection

This application does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information.

Specifically:

No user registration or login is required
No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
No contacts, messages, or personal files are accessed
No precise or continuous background location tracking is performed

The App is designed to function without collecting personal user data.

2. Location Permission

The App may request access to the user's location only once at startup in order to calculate accurate prayer times based on geographical position.

Location access is optional
Location is used only at the time of app launch
The App does not continuously track or store location data
Location data is not shared with any third parties

If the user denies location permission, prayer times may be calculated using approximate or default values.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertisements)

The App uses third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements.

These services may automatically collect and process non-personal data, including:

Advertising ID
Device information (device model, operating system version)
Anonymous usage and interaction data

This information is used only for:

Displaying relevant advertisements
Improving ad performance and user experience

The App does not control or access data collected by third-party advertisers.

For more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. API and Content Usage

The App retrieves Quran verses, translations (meal), and related religious content from trusted third-party APIs in real time.

No content is stored on developer-controlled servers
All data is fetched securely via HTTPS connections
No personal user data is transmitted to these APIs
5. Religious Content Disclaimer

The App provides Islamic content for educational and informational purposes only.

It does not represent any official Islamic authority, organization, or religious institution.

Users are encouraged to consult qualified scholars for religious rulings and interpretations.

6. Data Security

Since the App does not collect personal data, no personal data is stored, processed, or shared by the developer.

All communication with external services (APIs and ad networks) is conducted over secure HTTPS connections where applicable.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

If it is discovered that a child has provided personal information, appropriate action will be taken immediately.

8. Data Sharing and Sale

The App does not:

Collect user data
Sell user data
Share personal data with third parties
Transfer user data for commercial purposes

Third-party services (such as Google AdMob) may process non-personal data according to their own privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be published within the App or on this page. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
